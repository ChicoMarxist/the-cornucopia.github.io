About us

The Cornucopia is a project that in time, we hope to document every dish, food, and vegetable, and help those curious enough to find where they can find them.

We also wanted to have articles that got you straight to the point, Without having to read the author's life story about how swimming in a lake gave them all the skills they needed in life.

From Ducks to Watercress, Halva to Lollypops, You will find out what, and will find out where.
